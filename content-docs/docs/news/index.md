# News

This section contains News posts and drafts that are used for [https://roboconinc.com/news/](https://roboconinc.com/news/).

## Overview

The News section manages all content for the public-facing News & Events page on the RoboCon website. This includes both published articles and drafts in progress.

## Sections

- **Published**: News articles that are currently live on the website
- **Drafts**: News articles that are in development or pending publication

## Content Management

All news posts should include:
- Title
- Summary
- Content (full article)
- Image URL
- Published date
- Category
- Author
- Slug (URL-friendly identifier)
- Featured status
- Created date
