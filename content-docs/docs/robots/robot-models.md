# RoboCon Robot Models

RoboCon offers a comprehensive line of industrial robots designed for construction, material handling, service, and logistics applications. All models follow the Year/Make/Model naming convention and are part of the ROBOCON ecosystem.

## Model Naming Convention

RoboCon robots follow a standardized naming format:

- **Year**: 2025
- **Make**: RoboCon
- **Model**: [Family] [Base Type] [Payload]

Example: **2025 RoboCon Front Loader Tracked 300kg**

## Model Families

RoboCon robots are organized into the following families:

1. **Mini Crane** - Compact foldable crane systems
2. **Front Loader** - Material handling and transport loaders
3. **Excavator** - Earth-moving and digging operations
4. **Demolisher** - Demolition and heavy-duty operations
5. **Servicer** - Dual-arm service and maintenance robots
6. **Transporter** - Material transport and logistics
7. **Sheather** - Construction sheathing and material placement

## Base Configurations

Each model family is available in base mobility configurations:

- **Tracked**: Tracked mobility system for all-terrain operation
- **Wheeled**: Wheeled mobility system optimized for smooth surfaces

## Choosing the Right Model

When selecting a RoboCon robot model, consider:

1. **Application**: What tasks will the robot perform?
2. **Payload Requirements**: What is the maximum weight capacity needed?
3. **Terrain**: Will the robot operate on rough terrain (tracked) or smooth surfaces (wheeled)?
4. **Workspace**: Indoor vs. outdoor, confined vs. open spaces
5. **Autonomy Level**: Manual operation, semi-autonomous, or fully autonomous

For detailed specifications and capabilities of each model, refer to the individual robot documentation pages.
