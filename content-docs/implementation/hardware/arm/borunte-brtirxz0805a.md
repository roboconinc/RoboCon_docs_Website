## Robot Profile

### Introduction

BRTIRXZ0805A is a six axis collaborative robot with drag teaching function developed by BORUNTE, it has 5kg loading ability and 930mm arm span. It has collision detection and trajectory reproduction functions and also has the characteristics of safety, efficiency, intelligence, ease of use, flexibility, lightness, economic reliability, and low power consumption, greatly meeting the needs for human-machine cooperation. Its high sensitivity and fast response can be applied to high-density flexible production lines and can be used in product packaging, injection molding, loading and unloading, assembly and other operations, especially for the application needs of human-machine collaborative work.

High IP code: **IP50**. 

High accuracy: repeated positioning accuracy: **±0.05mm**

### Application Cases

1. Human-machine collaboration
2. Injection molding
3. Handling
4. Assembling
5. Loading and unloading
6. Packaging
