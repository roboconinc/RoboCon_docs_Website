# Cabinet Systems

Information about cabinet and drawer systems used in RoboCon robot systems.

## Overview

Cabinet and drawer systems provide housing and organization for control systems, computers, batteries, and other components in RoboCon robot systems. These systems are designed for industrial applications and provide protection, organization, and accessibility for critical robot components.

## Cabinet Types

### IL30 Cabinets

The IL30 cabinet system includes:
- **Front Cabinet**: Monitor and display systems
- **Back Cabinet**: Power distribution and control systems
- **Back Tall Cabinet**: Extended height version for additional components

### Drawer Systems

Drawer systems provide organized storage and access to:
- **Arm Control**: Robotic arm control systems
- **Base Control**: Base mobility control systems
- **Battery**: Power storage and management
- **Computer**: Computing systems and AI hardware
- **Boot**: System boot and initialization components

### Rack17 Systems

Rack17 drawer systems for rack-mounted configurations:
- **Computer**: Rack-mounted computer systems
- **Power**: Power distribution and management

## Components

Cabinet and drawer systems integrate various components including:
- Power converters and distribution
- Motor drivers and controllers
- PLC systems
- Computer systems
- Battery management
- Sensors and monitoring equipment
- Network switches and communication equipment
